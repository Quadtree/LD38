In Low Orbit you must race to the other side of a small planet and back! Controls:

## Controls: ##
* **WASD/Arrow Keys** - Accelerate/turn
* **R** - Reset to last checkpoint
* **Space** - Fire jets (Jetcrane only)
* **Escape** - Back to menu/exit
